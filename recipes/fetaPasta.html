Feta Cheese Pasta

Description

This is a feta cheese pasta recipe that you can cook super fast in the oven. It's a very basic dish but you can go as complex as your heart desires. You'll need a cast iron pan for this one.

Ingredients

  • 1 block of feta cheese
  • 1 container of cherry tomatoes
  • 2 cups of your favorite pasta
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • a sprig of basil
