Feta Cheese Pasta

Description

This is a feta cheese pasta recipe that you can cook super fast in the oven. It's a very basic dish but you can go as complex as your heart desires. You'll need a cast iron pan for this one.

Ingredients

  • 1 block of feta cheese
  • 1 container of cherry tomatoes
  • 2 cups of your favorite pasta
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • a sprig of basil

Steps

  1. First, heat your oven to 400 degrees and make sure your cast iron is oiled.
  2. Bring a pot of water to a boil and cook your pasta.
  3. Once oven is heated, place the block of feta in the center and fill the remaining space with the cherry tomatoes.
  4. Let the feta and tomatoes cook for about 25 minutes, or until the tomato juices are leaking.
  5. Take the cast iron out and use a spatula to crush the tomatoes and mix with the melted feta.
  6. Strain your pasta.
  7. Spread the tomato sauce over your pasta in a bowl. Serve with basil and salt. Enjoy!
