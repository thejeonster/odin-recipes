Burgers

Description

This is how to make delicious McDonald's style hamburgers. It's going to be the best burger that you've ever had in your life!

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Beef Patties
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Onions
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. First, get your pan piping hot and oil it with the olive oil.
  2. Salt and pepper your patties.
  3. Place your beef patties on the pan and cook to desired texture.
  4. While your patties are cooking, toast your buns, either with a toaster or on a grill.
  5. Once your patties are finished cooking, let them sit still for a few minutes.
  6. Slice your tomato, onion, and lettuce into thin strips.
  7. Apply ketchup across one side of the buns.
  8. Lay your patty, tomato, onion, lettuce, and cheese onto the patty.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
